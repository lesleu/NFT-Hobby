About Axie Infinity

Axie Infinity is a battle game made by Sky Mavis. Players need to own at least three creatures, called Axies, to be able to battle in the game. This strategy game has a strong tactical layer, because each Axie has its own unique powers. By combining the strengths of their Axies, players can develop a tactic which they can use to outsmart their opponents.

Players store each Axie creature in their own wallet as a non-fungible token (NFT). Sky Mavis has created their own sidechain for the Ethereum network, allowing players to exchange NFTs without having to worry about gas fees.

What is Play-to-Earn?

Play-to-Earn is the latest development in the games industry. It allows players to generate a steady stream of crypto income simply by playing the game.

Play-to-Earn gives gamers ownership over in-game assets and allowing them to increase their value by actively playing the game. By participating in the in-game economy, players are creating value for other players and the developers. In turn they are rewarded with in-game assets. These digital assets can be anything ranging from cryptocurrencies to in-game resources that are tokenized on the blockchain.

Players are now rewarded for putting time and effort into games. In Axie Infinity, players earn Small Love Potions (SLP). Players need these tokens to breed new Axies, but they can also sell the tokens to other players on the open marketplace.

Battling Your Axies

In Axie Infinity you can battle your Axies in 2 different game modes: Adventure and in the Arena. Adventure more you will battle bots that are Chimera - earning SLP and AXS. In the Arena, you get to battle your team of 3 against other players in the Arena Battle. The stats of your Axie's depend on their Body parrts. Mouths, Horns, Back, and Tails all come with an associated battle more while Eyes and Ears may confer passive boosts.

Breeding Axies

In Axie Infinity, you can also Breed your Axies. These offspring battle, breed new offspring, or can be sold on the marketplace. In order to manage the Axie population however, breeding has certain resource requirements.

Each Axie can be bred a maximum of 7 times. Breeds cost 1 AXS + Smooth Love Potions (SLP). Plus require gas fee which is variable based on usage of the Ethereum network.

Mixing two parents generate various offspring and are all based off of their Genetics. Each Axie has 6 body parts as well as a body shape. For each part, an Axie possesses 3 genes. A dominant (D), recessive (R1), and minor recessive gene (R2). As for how class is calculated, there are no recessive genes. Each baby has a 50% chance of inheriting each of their parent's class when born.

Lands

Land is something that Axie Infinity players can own and make as some-what of their "homebase". There are very limited land that can be owned and are sold in special sales.

By owning land, players get many benefits. Owner of land will be granted some "passive income" in a way that a lot of different structures will be buildable on those lands. Those structures will have many functionalities such as crafting ingredients, housing Axies, fishing, resource production, and more as the game develops further.
